# UI

Dark, professional. Deep slate (`zinc-950/900`), severity reds/ambers (`red-500`, `amber-500`, `yellow-400`), mono font for `clauseId` / `citationId`. Aesthetic: Palantir / Anduril, not consumer SaaS.

## Screens

**1. Landing.** H1 ("Review a contract in 10 seconds. Every risk is verified against the source.") · dropzone + "Try sample: DD-1155" · 4 trust badges (closed taxonomy · verified citations · exact-substring · audit trail).

**2. Analyzing.** 5-line staged progress, `framer-motion` row transitions: Parse → Concepts → Pass 1 (N/M) → Pass 2 → Pass 3.

**3. Results — 3 columns.**

| Left (clause list) | Center (clause detail) | Right (**proof panel** — headline) |
|---|---|---|
| Severity-grouped: `▼ HIGH (4)` `▼ MEDIUM (6)` `▼ LOW (3)`. Rows: `clauseId · category · score`. Footer: `Rejected (N)` drawer toggle. | Selected clause's full text with risky `<mark>` spans. Below: Finding cards (category, severity, score, reasoning, action, redline, "See proof →"). | When finding focused: verifier badges (✓ Quote ✓ Citation), cited reg in full, provenance (modelId, passType, promptHash, extractedAt). |

Tabs above columns: `Per-clause · Cross-ref · Hidden · Rejected`.
